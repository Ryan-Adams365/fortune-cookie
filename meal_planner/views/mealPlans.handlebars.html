Description

The classic universal Turing machine Langton's Ant has you give starting values to an ant that follows a simple instruction set for movement. Watch as your ant spirals out of control. Literally!

Features

  • Customize your board size and number of steps
  • Specify or randomize your starting location
  • Wrap-around edges!
